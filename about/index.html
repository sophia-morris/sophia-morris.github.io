About

Boring stuff

I am South African. I was born and raised in Cape Town and the Klein Karoo. I very quickly became aware that fashion was it for me, so I studied and received a BA of Fashion. I have worked in pretty much every aspect of the fashion industry (designing, manufacturing, styling, retail, customer care, etc) and still find myself wanting to do it all (except pattern-making, please do not ask me about pattern making).

Fun stuff

My focus and intrigue lies with the individual person and the conscious or unconscious fashion choices they make. I believe it is a direct reflection of their feeling of individuality. I have seen first hand how taking the time to choose pieces that are highly personal, can improve self identity and the gain of confidence in trusting their own agency.

I hope that wherever I land, or whatever I do in the fashion industry, aids the individual in finding their identity and enables the journey of self exploration.

This platform is for me to have an outlet for my good ideas, my bad ideas and for all the things I create in-between.
