Miscellaneous

Over the last 5 years, I have taken many photographs with several different film cameras. I have a talent for ransacking the charity store electronics section and riffling through friends and family’s homes for their forgotten camera collections.

The majority of the photographs have been taken on a family of point ‘n shoot cameras such as Kodak KB10, Pentax ESPIO 160 and Olypmus Trip AF S-2. For me, they are the best. I love the unpredictable outcome of each roll of film. I have found that even when you are absolutely sure you will get the photograph you wanted; the camera will overrule with what it decides. This unpredictability coincides with losing almost every roll of film for +/- 2 years before getting it developed.
